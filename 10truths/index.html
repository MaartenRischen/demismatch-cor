Humans need
  • A reason to get up.Something pulling you out of bed before anyone asks.
  • People who'd notice if you vanished.A few who'd come looking.
  • Respect from people who know you.Earned from faces you'll see again.
  • People who knew you before.Someone who remembers the old you and will be there for the next one.
  • To be touched.Skin, warmth, another body in the room.
  • A role only you can fill.A place that stays empty without you.
  • Real stakes.Things that get worse if you don't show up.
  • Effort that costs something.The hard part is the point. Remove it and the reward goes with it.
  • Work that leaves a mark.Effort that changed something you can point to.
  • Someone smaller to protect.A life that needs you to stay alive.
  • A village around your kid.One adult doing a five-person job is not a parenting style. It is a missing village.
  • To move the way you're built to.Hard physical work your day actually requires.
  • A future worth working toward.A tomorrow your effort actually changes.
Humans don't need
  • A feed that never ends.Built so the feeling of enough never arrives.
  • More followers.A bigger number, and still no one who would notice you gone.
  • An audience.Watched by thousands, known by none.
  • An AI that knows your name.A chatbot playing the friend you need.
  • A partner made of code.Your bonding goes out to something with no life on the other end of it.
  • A job sold as your identity.Purpose on loan from someone else's spreadsheet.
  • Points and streaks.Progress bars standing in for real consequences.
  • Everything made easy.Friction removed until nothing you do can fail or matter.
  • Achievements that unlock nothing.Badges and levels filling up while the world stays exactly the same.
  • Care from something that can't die.Comfort from a thing that acts like it cares and cannot.
  • Worlds full of fake people.Crowds of NPCs, not one of them anyone.
  • Exercise that's just a number.Your body was built to carry, build, and cover ground. Closing a ring is none of that.
  • A screen built to keep you watching.Engineered to hold the hours you would have spent building something.

Align AI to what humans need, not what they click.

Read the paper - The Optimizer Without a Target → Roadmap

Current alignment trains models on human preferences, and a preference is a mechanism firing, not necessarily what the person actually needs.

Alignment builds on the atlas in three tracks: a machine-readable spec any model reads at inference through one tool-call surface; steering, data curation, and evals against that spec as ground truth; and small models spec-grounded from pretraining forward.
