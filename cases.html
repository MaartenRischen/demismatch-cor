At a glance

Same operation, five times.

Each row: the presenting situation, the mechanism it is a signal from, the mismatch producing it, what the conventional reply is, and what Cor reads the signal as.

Case Mechanism Mismatch Conventional advice Cor prescription
01 · AI companion trap M3 Social Bonding Proxy bond fires the attachment system without reciprocal human co-regulation. Use the tool responsibly; set screen limits. AI must not occupy a Dunbar slot (DA9). Restore reliable human co-regulators; route AI toward human connection, not to replace it.
02 · Instagram depression M5 Status Monitoring Rank circuitry built for ~150 is forced to compare against millions of algorithmically selected lives. Work on self-esteem; reframe comparison cognitively. Restore a bounded reference group where position is stable, fair, and visible. Remove the global proxy; the signal is accurate to the environment.
03 · Parenting guilt M9 Care / Alloparenting Nuclear-family isolation substitutes for the distributed care network the architecture expects. Be gentler with yourself; hire help; read a parenting book. Guilt is an accurate signal that the system has no alloparental network. Build one. Architectural, not cultural.
04 · Grief without ritual M3 grief mode Communal mourning program of months compressed into a weekend of bereavement leave. Work through the stages; consider antidepressants if it persists. The signal is the architecture doing reorganization work. Restore time, communal presence, and ritual; do not medicate the update.
05 · The synthetic childhood M4 Play · M3 Attachment · M7 Circadian · DA7 Developmental Calibration Developmental windows calibrate to a world designed to hold attention, not to inputs the systems evolved to need. Set screen-time limits; pick better apps. Restore outdoor unstructured play, stable adult relationships, morning daylight, and movement. The windows close on whatever is present.
