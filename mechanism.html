Proxy gradient. The cue is identical at every level; only the resolving function changes. A proxy can fire the cue without supplying what resolves it.

Operationalization. Turning a mechanism into a coding test: a mismatch hypothesis, observable indicators, hit/miss/ambiguous coding criteria, falsification conditions, and an inter-rater reliability target. The first worked example exists for M3; the others follow as the specification is built.
