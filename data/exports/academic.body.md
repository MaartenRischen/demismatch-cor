## How to read this document

This document is a complete printout of the Cor atlas: a framework for locating modern human suffering within an evolutionary-functional architecture rather than within a presumption of individual defect. The central thesis is deflationary with respect to the disorder model and inflationary with respect to signal: a large fraction of aversive affective and physiological output is accurate biological signaling produced by adaptations operating correctly, but operating on inputs that fall outside the ranges within which those adaptations were calibrated to resolve. Suffering, on this account, is frequently diagnostic information about a mismatched environment, not noise to be suppressed and not evidence of broken design. Preferences are mechanism outputs, not ground truth.

### The derivation stack

The framework is built as a forced derivation in which each layer is constrained by the one beneath it.

- **Frames.** OF1 (Fitness Interface) holds that organismal access to the world is mediated by evolved fitness interfaces, not by guaranteed veridicality. OF2 (Signal-Default Epistemology) treats motivational-emotional outputs as prima facie informative about the organism's regulated conditions, by default. This is a diagnostic default, not a presumption of veridicality (OF1 still stands), and not a contraindication to symptom relief where indicated. The default is displaced by positive evidence of decoupling or direct physiological perturbation: developmental miscalibration carried forward, chronic dysregulation, acute organic disease or state shift (sleep deprivation, inflammation, endocrine perturbation, delirium), structural damage, and substance or medication effects.
- **Premises.** P1 (Inclusive Fitness) makes inclusive fitness - survival and reproduction - the loss function. P2 (Domain-Sensitive Interacting Adaptations) specifies an organism of evolved, domain-sensitive, interacting functional adaptations rather than a general-purpose learner. P3 (Systematic Mismatch) holds that modern environments often push these adaptations outside their expected or regulatable operating ranges.
- **Properties.** DA1: many aversive outputs are intelligible defensive signals under mismatch. DA2: human regulation is constitutively socially scaffolded, and social context is likely the highest-leverage cross-cutting input dimension. DA3: systems are recurrently coupled, so perturbations propagate and self-maintain. DA4: proxy cues can activate systems without meeting the conditions that normally regulate or terminate them. DA5: defensive systems err toward over-activation under asymmetric error costs. DA6: the architecture contains partially competing motivational programs and tradeoff structures. DA7: the architecture calibrates to developmental and ongoing input within evolved ranges. DA8: motivational programs have phylogenetically determined priority relations, with older survival-critical systems able to suppress or commandeer newer ones. DA9: mechanism resolution conditions are not fully interchangeable; cross-system buffering does not generally replace the specific resolution conditions of an impaired mechanism, and well-being is often bottlenecked by severe unresolved deficits in individual mechanisms rather than well described as a simple average across mechanism states.
- **Consequences.** DC1: chronic unresolved activation accumulates as allostatic load. DC2: markets can industrialize proxy exploitation of unmet regulatory needs. DC3: because cortical override of subcortical activation is metabolically expensive and chronic defensive activation downregulates the override machinery itself, interventions that change actual input conditions are frequently more durable and less effort-dependent than override-targeting interventions alone; the environment is therefore a primary, currently underweighted intervention layer. This does not preclude individual-level cognitive, pharmacological, or psychotherapeutic intervention where indicated; the claim concerns default leverage and systemic design, not exclusivity.

### How to read a proxy gradient

Each mechanism carries an ordinal ladder from full resolution to pure proxy. The load-bearing claim of every gradient is this: **the cue is identical at every level; only the resolving function changes.** A proxy can fire the cue without supplying what actually resolves the system. Reading the ladder top to bottom, the detectable cue stays roughly constant (or, in the cases flagged Kind B, the real input is progressively withdrawn or the terminating signal progressively disabled) while the conditions that would terminate the program drop away. The floor levels are where the cue fires and nothing is supplied - the open-loop state where activation cannot resolve. Verdict tags ('resolution met', 'partly resolved', 'fires, no resolution') record this independently of the level's ordinal name. Evidence tags (EVIDENCED, ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED, SPECULATIVE, CONTESTABLE) record the warrant for each rung; they are reported verbatim where present.

Each section also records the epistemic grade, tier, phylogenetic age, the forcing convergence and its named independent research streams with verbatim evidence-link counts, the cascade topology (which mechanisms a system suppresses, is suppressed by, and interacts with), and any sex-differentiated parameterization.

## M1 - Threat Management

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~500 Myr (all vertebrates). **Evidence link count:** 115. **Forced by convergence:** C10 (Threat-Detection via Ancient Subcortical Circuits).

The defensive system responding to threat cues. Includes an offensive mode (frustration-aggression) absorbed from the old M3 RAGE system. In the plainest terms: it detects danger and produces freeze, fight, or flee. **Resolution conditions:** safety established, threat eliminated or avoided.

C10 establishes that threat detection and defensive response are mediated by phylogenetically ancient subcortical circuits (amygdala, periaqueductal gray, hypothalamus) operating pre-attentively and capable of overriding cortical processing. The convergence is supported by Panksepp's FEAR-system neuroanatomy, the Öhman/Mineka backward-masking fear-module research program, and Nesse's clinical evolutionary psychiatry linking panic and anxiety to these defensive systems. Key researchers: Panksepp, LeDoux, Mobbs.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels along a resolvability/actionability axis; a true DA4 proxy appears only at the floor.

- **FULL** (resolution met): acute danger you can fight or flee and then escape
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): a real present threat resolved by a trusted other
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): chronic slow-burn real threat with no terminating action
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): abstract or anticipated threat with no physical target
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): engineered supernormal outrage/threat media [tag: CONTESTABLE] [note: CONTESTABLE]

**Mismatch prediction:** modern threats - debt, emails, social media, status anxiety - cannot be fought or fled. The system stays on.

### Cascade topology

Suppresses M2, M4, M14, M10, and M6. Suppressed by M3. No interactions recorded.

## M2 - Pursuit / Exploration

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~500 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 67. **Forced by convergence:** C6 (Wanting-Liking Dissociation as Proxy Hijack).

The general-purpose seeking system. In a scope change from v3, sexual motivation has been removed and now lives in M14; M2's resolution conditions narrow to engagement with real-world challenges producing tangible outcomes, goal progress, skill building, and resource acquisition. **Resolution conditions:** engagement with real challenges producing tangible outcomes.

C6 establishes that motivational pursuit ('wanting', mediated by mesolimbic dopamine) is dissociable from hedonic satisfaction ('liking'), creating an open-loop vulnerability in which proxy cues can activate the SEEKING system without providing the conditions that normally terminate or resolve it. Established independently by Berridge & Robinson's incentive-salience neuroscience, Tinbergen's supernormal-stimuli ethology, and Panksepp's SEEKING-circuit affective neuroscience. Key researchers: Panksepp, Berridge.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. The cue is constant (SEEKING/wanting); the ladder is ordered by tangible outcome plus consummatory-liking closure supplied, not by cue strength.

- **FULL** (resolution met): engaging a real challenge that yields a real outcome [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): knowledge work with a real but abstracted, delayed outcome
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): variable-reward app/feed with intermittent real payoff
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): infinite novelty, content scroll/idle browsing
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): machine gambling engineered for time-on-device with guaranteed net loss [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]

Note: the most contestable ordering is MIXED-app vs MINIMAL-scroll.

**Mismatch prediction:** variable-reward apps fire SEEKING without resolution. Open loop.

## M3 - Social Bonding

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~200 Myr (mammalian). **Evidence link count:** 192. **Forced by convergence:** C5 (Socially Scaffolded Regulation via Attachment).

The attachment and co-regulation system. Includes the grief mode absorbed from the old M9 GRIEF system. It runs in two states: security (enables broadly) and grief (suppresses broadly). It forms bonds with other humans and produces pain-grade distress when they are gone.

**Resolution conditions:** reliable human co-regulation through physical presence, operating through nested social layers - ~5 intimate co-regulators (primary emotional/physical support), ~15 sympathy group, ~50 close social partners, and ~150 coherent face-to-face relationships (the Dunbar number, the outer cognitive ceiling). Each layer represents qualitatively different relationship types with different obligations and investment requirements. The innermost ~5 provide the majority of social regulation; the critical variable at this layer is reliability, not count. The ~150 outer figure is an illustrative central tendency, not a hard limit: its derivation from the primate neocortex-ratio regression is contested (Lindenfors, Wartel & Lind 2021), while the layered structure and its low-hundreds clustering are separately observed in phone, Christmas-card, and Facebook data.

C5 establishes that human regulation is constitutively socially scaffolded: the organism's default expectation is proximity to trusted co-regulators, and this scaffolding is architectural rather than optional. Established independently by Coan's Social Baseline Theory fMRI program, Bowlby's attachment theory, Hrdy's cooperative-breeding thesis, Dunbar's social-brain hypothesis, and Holt-Lunstad's mortality meta-analysis showing a 50% survival advantage from strong social relationships. Key researchers: Bowlby, Panksepp, Coan, Hrdy.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels, ordered by real co-regulatory function supplied, not by amount of contact.

- **FULL** (resolution met): reliable physically co-present inner circle
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): real bonded inner circle held at a distance, remote contact
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): large mediated network routing only partly to real co-presence
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): one-sided parasocial attachment to a non-reciprocating figure
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): AI companion engineered to simulate a reciprocating bond [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED-theoretical on ext595]

Note (off-gradient): isolated/solo living is empty-shelf deprivation, not a rung on this ladder.

**Mismatch prediction:** nuclear family, isolated living, and mediated contact substituting for physical presence.

### Cascade topology

Suppresses M1, M2, M4, M10, and M6. Suppressed by nothing recorded. Interacts with M14.

### Sex differentiation

## M4 - Social Calibration / Play

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~200 Myr (mammalian, absent in reptiles). **Evidence link count:** 55. **Forced by convergence:** C2, C4, and C12.

The play system develops social skills; it is absent in threatened or ill animals. It learns social rules through rough-and-tumble play and requires real playmates. **Resolution conditions:** age-matched, self-directed, unsupervised play, physical.

This mechanism is forced by three converging streams. C2 (Domain-Sensitive Organism Architecture) holds the organism contains evolved, domain-sensitive, interacting functional adaptations rather than a general-purpose learner, supported independently by Tooby & Cosmides' design-principle derivation, Panksepp's conserved subcortical affective-motivational systems, and Bowlby's attachment-as-primary-motivational-system framework. C4 (Phylogenetic Conservation of Subcortical Affective Systems) establishes that the core affective-motivational systems (SEEKING, FEAR, RAGE, CARE, PANIC/GRIEF, PLAY, LUST) are anatomically and functionally conserved across mammals and extend into birds for specific systems, established independently by Panksepp's deep-brain-stimulation and cross-species neuroscience, Bowlby's attachment cross-mammalian homology, and Hrdy's cross-primate parental/alloparental evidence. C12 (Developmental Calibration within Evolved Ranges) holds that specific systems calibrate during specific developmental windows to inputs within evolved parameters, with out-of-parameter inputs producing persistent miscalibration, established independently by Bowlby's attachment developmental calibration, McEwen's HPA-axis calibration neuroendocrinology, and Felitti's ACE Study dose-response evidence. Key researchers: Panksepp, Pellis & Pellis, Gray.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. The cue is approximately a constant PLAY/SEEKING social-joy pull, fired in attenuated or substitute form at lower screen levels; the ladder is ordered by how much real embodied reciprocity with an identity-bearing partner survives.

- **FULL** (resolution met): age-matched, self-directed, unsupervised physical play with peers [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): adult-structured supervised physical activity, organized sports
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): screen-mediated multiplayer play with real peers
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): solitary single-player screen play, no reciprocating partner
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): engineered companion-AI / supernormal variable-reward 'play' product [tag: ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED] [note: architecture-derived]

Note: the most contestable ordering is screen-mediated-real-peers (MIXED) ranked above solitary scripted game (MINIMAL).

**Mismatch prediction:** structured activities replacing free play; screens replacing bodies; play deprivation.

### Cascade topology

Suppresses nothing recorded. Suppressed by M1 and M3. Interacts with M2.

## M5 - Status Monitoring

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~50-100 Myr (primates and social mammals). **Evidence link count:** 88. **Forced by convergence:** C2, C7, C9, and C13.

Rank regulation: shame, pride, submission, defeat, rank vigilance. It is not optional and runs continuously, monitoring where one stands relative to others. **Resolution conditions:** an acceptable position in a ~150-person reference group, with earned prestige preferred over coerced dominance (the Henrich & Gil-White distinction, which needs explicit treatment).

Four convergences support this mechanism. C2 (Domain-Sensitive Organism Architecture), supported by Tooby & Cosmides, Panksepp, and Bowlby. C7 (Adverse Experience Cascading Dose-Response), establishing that chronic or developmental adversity cascades across physiological systems in a dose-response manner with effects that propagate and self-maintain across metabolic, immune, neural, and behavioral domains, established independently by Felitti's ACE Study clinical epidemiology, McEwen's allostatic-load neuroendocrinology, and Panksepp's cross-system inhibition evidence. C9 (Allostatic Load Accumulation), establishing cumulative multi-organ damage from chronic unresolved stress activation, partly reversible by environmental correction, established independently by McEwen's neuroendocrinology of allostasis, Felitti's ACE dose-response epidemiology, and Sapolsky's primate stress physiology linking hierarchy type to cortisol and downstream pathology. C13 (Aversive Outputs as Intelligible Defensive Signals), established independently by Nesse's 'good reasons for bad feelings', Panksepp's affective neuroscience, and Fehr & Gächter's finding that moralistic outrage is the proximate mechanism for prosocial norm enforcement rather than a dysregulation. Key researchers: Gilbert, Price, Mazur, Sapolsky, Marmot.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. The cue is approximately a constant comparison/ranking signal, ordered primarily by real standing supplied in a real knowable group, with prestige > dominance quality folded in at the top.

- **FULL** (resolution met): earned prestige in a small face-to-face group [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): coerced/unstable dominance rank in a real group [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): status-striving in a large impersonal society [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): global mediated comparison by follower/like counts [tag: ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED] [note: architecture-derived]
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): purchased status signals, luxury-brand display [tag: ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED] [note: architecture-derived]

Note: the most contestable point is the floor ordering of purchased-signals (ZERO) vs social-media comparison (MINIMAL).

**Mismatch prediction:** global comparison via social media, with millions of "peers," producing a status-defeat cascade.

## M6 - Controllability / Agency

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~200 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 38. **Forced by convergence:** C8 and C13.

The vmPFC-DRN controllability circuit. Critically, learned helplessness is the DEFAULT; agency is the override. This mechanism is central to breaking cascades. It tracks whether actions produce results; the default is passivity, and agency must be learned. **Resolution conditions:** genuine action-outcome feedback and decision latitude.

C8 (Error Management Asymmetry in Defensive Systems) establishes that defensive systems evolved under asymmetric error costs err reliably toward over-activation (false-positive) because the cost of missing a true threat historically exceeded the cost of reacting to a non-threat - established independently by Nesse's smoke-detector principle, Haselton & Buss's Error Management Theory, and the Öhman/Mineka fear-module visual-attention program. C13 (Aversive Outputs as Intelligible Defensive Signals) is established independently by Nesse, Panksepp, and Fehr & Gächter. Key researchers: Maier & Seligman.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. This is a Kind A gradient, but the cue is detection of response-outcome contingency rather than a constant appetitive drive; the ladder is ordered by real causal efficacy over fitness-relevant outcomes supplied.

- **FULL** (resolution met): decision latitude over consequential, visibly-feedbacking outcomes [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): high demand with reduced but real decision latitude [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): abstract/surveilled/screen-mediated work where effort doesn't visibly register [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): chronically uncontrollable real environment, learned helplessness [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): engineered simulated-agency loop (slot machine/game/dashboard) firing the contingency cue with zero real consequence [tag: ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED] [note: architecture-derived]

Note: this is dual-axis. PARTIAL to MINIMAL withdraw real contingency; ZERO substitutes a false one. The most contestable ordering is MINIMAL vs ZERO.

**Mismatch prediction:** abstract work where effort doesn't visibly register; rigid hierarchies; bureaucratic systems.

## M7 - Circadian Regulation

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~3 Byr (cyanobacteria onward). **Evidence link count:** 20. **Forced by convergence:** C3, C9, and C12.

The SCN master clock. It gates metabolism, immunity, cognition, and mood. The mismatch is usually one of timing or quality, not duration. It synchronizes every system to the light-dark cycle and is the master timing architecture. **Resolution conditions:** dark-cued sleep onset, bright daylight exposure, and a consistent rhythm.

C3 (Systematic EEA-Modern Environment Mismatch) holds that modern post-agricultural, post-industrial environments push evolved adaptations outside their expected operating ranges in systematic and predictable ways, established independently by Eaton & Konner's discordance hypothesis, the Li/van Vugt/Colarelli mismatch-identification framework, Boyd & Richerson's gene-culture coevolution constraint (matched must include cultural scaffolding), and Henrich's WEIRD empirical diagnostic showing Western populations anchor the extreme end of the cross-cultural distribution. C9 (Allostatic Load Accumulation) and C12 (Developmental Calibration within Evolved Ranges) co-force, with their named streams as above. Key researchers: Walker, Roenneberg, Czeisler.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. This is a Kind B gradient: not a constant-cue hijack but a case where the entraining light signal itself degrades and is finally inverted; ordered by per-individual signal degradation. All five are EVIDENCED.

- **FULL** (resolution met): outdoor light-dark cycle with dark-cued sleep
- **ATTENUATED** (resolution met): indoor daytime light with a mostly-dark, dimly-lit night
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): correctly-phased clock overridden by a misaligned social schedule / social jetlag
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): shift work and always-on connectivity with the dark cue largely gone
- **DEFEATED** (fires, no resolution): evening blue-light screens actively mis-setting the clock, false daylight at biological night

Note: the most contestable points are the social-jetlag (PARTIAL) vs shift-work (MINIMAL) ordering, and whether screens' signal-inversion is the true floor.

**Mismatch prediction:** electric light, blue screens, shift work, caffeine.

### Cascade topology

Suppresses nothing recorded. Suppressed by nothing recorded. No interactions recorded.

## M8 - Immune Regulation

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~600 Myr (all multicellular). **Evidence link count:** 37. **Forced by convergence:** C3, C7, and C9.

The cytokine signaling system. It overrides voluntary intention and is the hub connecting inflammation to depression via the DA3 cascade. In plainest terms, it produces sickness behavior: inflammation shuts everything else down to fight infection. **Resolution conditions:** pathogen cleared, inflammation resolved.

C3 (Systematic EEA-Modern Environment Mismatch), with its named streams as above. C7 (Adverse Experience Cascading Dose-Response), established independently by Felitti, McEwen, and Panksepp. C9 (Allostatic Load Accumulation), established independently by McEwen, Felitti, and Sapolsky. Key researchers: Dantzer, Danese, Irwin.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. This is a Kind B gradient: the trigger drifts from a real clearable pathogen toward psychosocial/lifestyle signals with nothing to clear, while the inflammatory off-switch degrades; ordered by how achievable clear-to-resolve-to-baseline remains. All five are EVIDENCED.

- **FULL** (resolution met): acute infection/injury fought off and fully resolved
- **ATTENUATED** (resolution met): recurrent acute stress resolving each time but with incomplete recovery
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): sustained lifestyle drivers (sedentarism, poor diet, sleep loss) keeping inflammation simmering
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): chronic social isolation/status stress defeating the inflammatory off-switch
- **DEFEATED** (fires, no resolution): self-sustaining chronic low-grade inflammation, allostatic-load collapse

Note: the most contestable point is lifestyle-drivers (PARTIAL) ranked above social-isolation (MINIMAL) - these could legitimately swap or collapse.

**Mismatch prediction:** chronic low-grade inflammation from diet, sedentarism, and social isolation.

## M9 - Care / Alloparenting

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~200 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 64. **Forced by convergence:** C1, C4, C5, and C12.

The parental and alloparental care system, running on oxytocin/prolactin circuits. The architecture expects distributed care - a village, not solo parenting. **Resolution conditions:** a distributed care network rather than solo or dyadic parenting. Traditional-society data typically show multiple non-parent caregivers (~4+); the critical resolving variable is the presence and continuity of real alloparental supply (care plus provisioning), NOT bare kinship.

Four convergences force this mechanism. C1 (Inclusive Fitness as Loss Function), demonstrated independently by Darwinian selection theory, Hamilton's kin-selection formalism, and experimental altruistic-punishment findings. C4 (Phylogenetic Conservation of Subcortical Affective Systems), established by Panksepp, Bowlby, and Hrdy. C5 (Socially Scaffolded Regulation via Attachment), established by Coan, Bowlby, Hrdy, Dunbar, and Holt-Lunstad. C12 (Developmental Calibration within Evolved Ranges), established by Bowlby, McEwen, and Felitti. Key researchers: Hrdy, Panksepp, Kenkel.

## M10 - Movement / Regulatory

**Grade:** strongly_supported. **Tier:** 2. **Phylogenetic age:** ~500 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 29. **Forced by convergence:** C3 and C9.

Physical activity functioning as a zeitgeber, anti-inflammatory, and mood regulator. Movement is not merely exercise: it activates repair mechanisms throughout the organism. **Resolution conditions:** movement that is part of daily life rather than set aside as 'exercise'. Hunter-gatherers typically moved 9-15 km a day as a side effect of how they lived.

C3 (Systematic EEA-Modern Environment Mismatch), with its named streams as above. C9 (Allostatic Load Accumulation), established by McEwen, Felitti, and Sapolsky. Key researchers: Pontzer, Lieberman, Ratey.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. This is a Kind B gradient that inverts the usual proxy story - the real input is withdrawn, not a false cue fired; ordered by how much of the all-day muscle-activity distribution is supplied. All five are EVIDENCED.

- **FULL** (resolution met): all-day incidental movement of foraging life, locomotion plus active rest postures
- **ATTENUATED** (resolution met): a physically active job, farming/manual labour/walking-heavy trade
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): mostly sedentary day broken up by walking, standing and fidgeting / incidental NEAT
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): sedentary day plus a discrete gym/exercise session, a partial re-supply not a false cue
- **DEFEATED** (fires, no resolution): chair-bound sedentarism with essentially no movement

Note: the most contestable point is the gym (MINIMAL) ranked below incidental all-day NEAT (PARTIAL) - this would swap on a fitness/mortality rather than distribution axis.

**Mismatch prediction:** sedentarism, with gym sessions as partial compensation.

## M11 - Cooperation / Alliance

**Grade:** strongly_supported. **Tier:** 2. **Phylogenetic age:** ~2-5 Myr (humans and great apes). **Evidence link count:** 73. **Forced by convergence:** C11 (Reciprocity, Norm Enforcement, and Coalition Architecture).

Reciprocity plus norm enforcement plus alliance dynamics. This mechanism absorbs the old M10 (Cooperation) and M11 (Coalitional). Its Tier 2 placement reflects epistemic honesty: no single convergence forces a dedicated mechanism, but four converging streams strongly support it. In plainest terms: reciprocal exchange, cheater detection, and coalitional identity.

**Resolution conditions:** a trusted reciprocal network with norm enforcement within a group small enough for direct reputation tracking (on the order of ~150 - the same low-hundreds ceiling that bounds M3; the precise figure is contested per Lindenfors 2021). Norm enforcement requires repeated direct interaction where defectors can be identified, remembered, and sanctioned. Beyond that ceiling, cooperation requires institutional scaffolding (formal rules, third-party enforcement, shared fictions) rather than direct reciprocity. There is no shared fiction at the direct-reciprocity layer.

C11 establishes that humans have specialized architecture for reciprocal exchange, cheater detection, norm enforcement, and coalitional identity, supported independently by Fehr & Gächter's experimental altruistic-punishment findings, Boehm's reverse-dominance coalition dynamics in hunter-gatherer anthropology, and Tomasello's shared-intentionality comparative-cognition research. Key researchers: Trivers, Boehm, Fehr & Gächter, Tomasello.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. The cue is approximately a constant alliance/reciprocity machinery. FULL to PARTIAL is resolution-via-prosthesis - institutions re-supply the same function beyond ~150 - so proxy failure begins at MIXED; ordered by how much enforceable reciprocal function reaches a real reputation-bearing counterparty.

- **FULL** (resolution met): trusted reciprocal network under ~150 with direct reputation tracking and norm enforcement [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): institutionally-scaffolded cooperation beyond ~150, formal rules / third-party enforcement / shared fictions [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): market exchange among strangers backed by gameable reputation systems
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): coalition-signaling and parasocial alliance via symbolic in-group markers
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): anonymous one-shot transactions and AI 'allies' with no reputation and no shared narrative [tag: ARCHITECTURE-DERIVED] [note: architecture-derived]

Note: the most contestable point is the PARTIAL/MIXED boundary - where proxy failure begins.

**Mismatch prediction:** anonymous transactions, ghosting, no shared narrative.

## M12 - Contamination Avoidance

**Grade:** strongly_supported. **Tier:** 2. **Phylogenetic age:** ~300 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 12. **Forced by convergence:** C2 and C8.

Three-domain disgust (pathogen, sexual, moral): the behavioral immune system. Pathogen avoidance extended to moral and social contamination. **Resolution conditions:** clean food, clean social environment, moral disgust contained. (Note: the 'moral disgust contained' clause is flagged CONTESTED.)

## M13 - Energy Regulation

**Grade:** strongly_supported. **Tier:** 2. **Phylogenetic age:** ~500 Myr. **Evidence link count:** 34. **Forced by convergence:** C3, C6, and C9.

Pontzer's constrained energy model: energy is redistributed, not simply burned. The mechanism comprises hunger, satiety, and constrained total energy expenditure. **Resolution conditions:** effortful, diverse, seasonal whole foods; metabolic variety.

C3 (Systematic EEA-Modern Environment Mismatch), with its named streams as above. C6 (Wanting-Liking Dissociation as Proxy Hijack), established by Berridge & Robinson, Tinbergen, and Panksepp. C9 (Allostatic Load Accumulation), established by McEwen, Felitti, and Sapolsky. Key researchers: Pontzer, Keys.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels. This is a Kind B gradient: the satiety / intake-termination signal itself is progressively disabled then actively defeated, not a constant cue hijacked. The scale is food-processing intensity, ordered by how much regulatory function the food permits.

- **FULL** (resolution met): effortful, diverse, seasonal whole foods [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]
- **ATTENUATED** (resolution met): minimally processed traditional whole foods
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): conventionally processed energy-dense food
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): energy-dense palatable food optimized for crave
- **DEFEATED** (fires, no resolution): ultra-processed bliss-point food, an engineered supernormal stimulus [tag: EVIDENCED] [note: EVIDENCED]

Note: endpoints are EVIDENCED, the middle three architecture-derived. The most contestable point is whether the ATTENUATED/PARTIAL/MINIMAL middle is three real tiers or a bimodal whole-vs-ultra-processed split.

**Mismatch prediction:** ultra-processed foods defeating satiety signals.

## M14 - Reproductive Motivation

**Grade:** forced. **Tier:** 1. **Phylogenetic age:** ~500 Myr sexual, ~200 Myr pair bonding. **Evidence link count:** 58. **Forced by convergence:** C14 (Reproductive Motivation as Distinct Architecture).

Fisher's three-system model, independent from M2 pursuit and running on the HPG axis, not just brain circuits. The three components - desire, attraction, and pair bonding - can work independently of one another, and this system shapes much more of human behavior than reproduction itself. **Resolution conditions:** sexual fulfillment within a pair bond, plus pair-bond stability, plus reproductive success.

C14 establishes that reproductive motivation (desire, attraction, pair-bonding) is a distinct architectural system independent of general-purpose pursuit, with sex-differentiated substrates and a characteristic proxy gradient from real partner to pure proxy - established independently by Panksepp's LUST-circuit affective neuroscience, Buss's sexual strategies theory and sex-differentiated mate preferences research, and Haselton/Buss's error-management prediction of sex-differentiated perceptual biases around mating cues. Key researchers: Panksepp, Fisher, Buss, Trivers, Hrdy.

### Proxy gradient

Five levels, top (need met) to bottom (cue fires, nothing supplied).

- **FULL** (resolution met): real partner
- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): attenuated real / LDR
- **MIXED** (partly resolved): partial proxy / dating app to real meeting
- **MINIMAL** (fires, no resolution): pure proxy soft / romance fiction / parasocial
- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): pure proxy hard / pornography / AI sexual

**Mismatch prediction:** pornography, dating apps, delayed reproduction, social media mate-value comparison.

### Cascade topology

Suppresses M6 and M11. Suppressed by M1, M8, and M9. Interacts with M3, M5, and M2.

### Sex differentiation

This is a sex-differentiated implementation: the same system with two parameterizations. The **female-typical** parameterization is VMH-centered, with estrous cycling, preference for resource/commitment signals, and emphasis on pair-bond security. The **male-typical** parameterization is POA-centered, with tonic testosterone, attention to fertility/accessibility cues, and emphasis on sexual variety.

## R1 - Touch

**Grade:** moderate. **Tier:** 3. **Phylogenetic age:** ~200 Myr (mammalian). **Evidence link count:** 18. **Forced by convergence:** C4 and C5.

R1 is NOT a motivational system. It is a regulatory input to M3, carried by C-tactile afferents - a regulatory channel, not a motivation. **Resolution conditions:** regular physical contact with trusted others.

C4 (Phylogenetic Conservation of Subcortical Affective Systems), established by Panksepp, Bowlby, and Hrdy. C5 (Socially Scaffolded Regulation via Attachment), established by Coan, Bowlby, Hrdy, Dunbar, and Holt-Lunstad. Key researchers: McGlone, Field.

- **PARTIAL** (partly resolved): affective touch from a non-intimate caregiver / massage

- **ZERO** (fires, no resolution): synthetic/haptic/AI C-tactile stimulation, zero relational embedding [tag: SPECULATIVE] [note: SPECULATIVE/future-conditional]

**Mismatch prediction:** touch-starved modern environments. Kangaroo-care evidence shows large developmental and autonomic gains from skin-to-skin care.
