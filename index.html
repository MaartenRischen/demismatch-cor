The framework

An evidence-graded atlas of what a human being needs.

Modern human suffering is accurate biological signaling in a mismatched environment, not disorder in the individual. The fix is changing the inputs so deep systems stop signaling, not patching the human.

Preferences are mechanism outputs, not ground truth. A proxy can fire the cue without supplying the function that would resolve it.
